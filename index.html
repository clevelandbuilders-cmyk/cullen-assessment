Garage Entry Door
Fire Rated
Garage Entry — Fire-Rated Door

The door between your garage and living space is required by code to be fire-rated. We’ll supply and install a UL-listed fire-rated door slab and properly sized frame, ensuring your home meets current building code while looking clean and finished. A brass kick plate on the garage side adds durability where the door sees the most wear.

UL-listed fire-rated door slab — code compliant
Properly sized frame — depth matched to wall
Handle and deadbolt on right side (interior view)
Brass kick panel — garage side
White interior and exterior finish
Interior 5.5” flat stock casing with decorative backband
Door confirmed to self-close per fire code
Fire-rated doors require a specific UL label and self-closing hardware to meet code. We’ll confirm the exact specification for your municipality before ordering.
